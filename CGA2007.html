POPTROPICA

Poptropica is a island exploring game with problem solving quests. Each Island has different themes and storylines that players can complete. Created in 2007, Poptropica became popular in the years 2011 and 2012. It is a multiplayer online game that was made for kids ages 6-15. One of the main developers and designers is Jeff Kinney who is popularly known for his late work, Diary of a Wimpy kid. Poptropica is still playable today on Cool Math Games.

GIRLS GO GAMES

Girls Go Games website was the home of many Barbie, dress up, makeup, nails, spa, and many more games growing up. These two websites have many solo online games that kids would usually play alone or when hanging out with friends. The website has a variety of games that were simple but addictive to play. Some popular games on Girls go Games would be Sara's Cooking Class. Shopaholic, Love Tester, Makeover games and more. The website is still active and has a variety of games to still play.

AKINATOR

Akinator is a person guessing game where the computer genie would ask a series of questions until it's able to guess of the person we are thinking about. The questions eliminate as many character options, based on the players yes no or maybe responses. The game has such a large database that the game became very popular for being able to guess who the player is thinking of. It was a very entertaining game that I'd play a lot with my friends growing up.

PURBLE PLACE

The memory of using the home computer or going down the the schools computer room and playing Purble Place. The game has 3 different game modes that you can find when clicking on the different buildings. The school building on the left has a tile matching game called Purble Pairs, The cake factory in the middle has a cake making game called Comfy Cakes and the final shop on the right is the guessing game called Purble Shop, through deduction you guess what character behind the curtain looks like.
The game was developed by Oberon Media for the new Microsoft Vista. When Microsoft Windows 7 was released, the game was also included on there. Purble place can only be played on those Windows and was not included in future releases.
