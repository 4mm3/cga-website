POPTROPICA

INFO

Poptropica is a island exploring game with problem solving quests. It is a multiplayer online game that was made for kids ages 6-15. One of the main developers and designers is Jeff Kinney who is popularly known for his late work, Diary of a Wimpy kid. Created in 2007, Poptropica became popular in the years 2011 and 2012.

GIRLS GO GAMES

INFO

Girls Go Games website was the home of many Barbie, dress up, makeup, nails, spa, and many more games growing up. These two websites have many solo online games that kids would usually play alone or when hanging out with friends.

AKINATOR

INFO

Akinator is a person guessing game where the computer genie would ask a series of questions until it's able to guess of the person we are thinking about.

PURBLE PLACE

INFO

The memory of using the home computer or going down the the schools computer room and playing Purble Place. The game has 3 different game modes that you can find when clicking on the different buildings. The school building on the left has a tile matching game called Purble Pairs, The cake factory in the middle has a cake making game called Comfy Cakes and the final shop on the right is the guessing game called Purble Shop, through deduction you guess what character behind the curtain looks like. The game was developed by Oberon Media for the new Microsoft Vista. When Microsoft Windows 7 was released, the game was also included on there. Purble place can only be played on those Windows and was not included in future releases.

" I remember in 2008 when I was in kindergarten, my class would often have class trips to the schools computer lab and we were allowed to play different computer games. The game I'd always play was Purble Place. My personal favourite game mode was the cake making game." Emma
