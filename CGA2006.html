ROBLOX

This very popular web and game creation game was released in 2006. Creators were able to to use Roblox to create, design and upload their games. In the beginning, simple games were created such as obstacle courses (known as Obby), hangout rooms, role-playing games, tycoons and more. Now in 2024, these type of game still do exist, but game created on Roblox have become more complex and higher quality. The game is still playable and has millions of players.
