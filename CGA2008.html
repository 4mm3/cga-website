PIXIE HOLLOW

INFO

Pixie Hollow us a MMORPG (massive multiplayer online role playing game) based on the Disney Fairies. Developled by Disney Interactive studios, the game was released around the time when the 2008 Tinkerbell film was released. Disney closed game was closed in 2013 but a rewritten version (not owned by Disney) has been recently created and is called We the Pixies. "When I played Pixie Hollow as a kid, my fairy had a animal talent and I remember making my fairy have brown hair and brown eyes to look like me. I loved exploring the Pixie Hollow world and playing minigames." EMM.F
