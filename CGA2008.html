PIXIE HOLLOW

Pixie Hollow us a MMORPG (massive multiplayer online role playing game) based on the Disney Fairies. Developled by Disney Interactive studios, the game was released around the time when the 2008 Tinkerbell film was released. Disney closed game was closed in 2013 but a rewritten version (not owned by Disney) has been recently created and is called We the Pixies.

MOSHI MONSTERS

Moshi Monsters was a MMO virtual pet monster game that allowed players to adopt and take care of a pet monster. The game has 6 types of monsters you can adopt called Katsumas, Poppets, Diavols, Furis, Luvlis and Zommers. The game allows monster customization, games and exploring the games virtual world, Monstro City.

FANTAGE

Created in 2008, Fantage offers a variety of games and actives, character customization, player interactions, home decoration and more. It was an online virtual world game that was created for kids and pre-teens. Fantage closed down 10 years later in 2018 and there is no rewritten version.

WIZARD 101

Wizard 101 is a MMORPG (massively multiplayer online role-playing game) about wizards and magic. The game is set in a fantasy world named Spiral and players play student wizards that attending Ravenwood School of Magical Arts. The game includes a card system that allows players to casting spells and use abilities in PvP or PvE gameplay.
